Who am I?

My name is Iris Loret, and I was born in 2003. I'm currently studying Applied Computer Science at Thomas More in Geel. I chose for this English program because it is something that seemed like a new challenge and also an interesting experience to do it in English. At first, I wasn't sure what I wanted to study, but I'm glad that I chose this program because I learned so many new things, and it is interesting. It also taught me that if you want something, you can do it.

I love creative hobbies like making things with clay or epoxy but also running because it really clears my mind. I also like traveling and discovering new places.

Dreams for the future

I'm not really sure what I want to do later. I might study something else after this bachelor but that's also not sure yet. I do like coding and working with data, it's fascinating to see what you can make with a piece of code.

I hope I can work in a company where I feel good and valued and where I can grow. I hope that I will realise over the next few years what I want to do as a job.

I think I will be an ambitious employee that will definitly work hard.
